# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

OneGoods Studio serves shoppers who want small, tactile, cute objects for desk breaks, gifting, and everyday mood repair. They may arrive from Shopee, 小红书, Instagram, or a shared product link. They are not looking for maker process first; they want to quickly understand the object, how it feels, whether it is cute enough to keep or gift, and where to buy it.

## Product Purpose

OneGoods Studio is a brand and commerce hub for stress-relief / emotional-value 3D printed goods. The site should make the product category instantly clear, show the current test picks, explain the small-batch 3D printed nature, and route visitors to the right purchase channel.

Success looks like visitors understanding the offer in one screen, clicking into a product, following a Shopee / 小红书 / Instagram purchase path, or joining the restock and launch list.

## Brand Personality

Tactile, cheerful, and neatly crafted. The voice should feel warm and clear, not poetic for its own sake. Cute is welcome, but the brand should still feel like a real consumer goods label rather than a personal hobby page.

## Anti-references

- A personal creator side-project page centered on the founder.
- Generic AI landing pages with purple gradients, decorative glass cards, and vague claims.
- Hard industrial maker pages that lead with engineering instead of the product experience.
- Large marketplace layouts with too many SKUs and no curation.
- Overly sentimental copy that hides what the product actually does.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with the object: every important section should help the visitor picture, touch, or buy the goods.
2. Sell the feeling, then explain the making: hand feel and mood value come before 3D printing process details.
3. Keep the assortment small and intentional: test picks should feel curated, not empty or unfinished.
4. Make buying paths obvious: product pages should clearly separate Shopee, 小红书, Instagram, and launch reminders.
5. Preserve a polished small-batch tone: handmade texture is a feature, but the presentation should feel trustworthy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for body text, buttons, forms, and focus states. Keep motion light and nonessential. Touch targets should be at least 44px high on mobile. Product copy should avoid technical assumptions about 3D printing and explain care notes plainly.
